MBA from Clarkson University

Clarkson University is one of the renowned universities for pursuing an MBA programme in the US. It offers the facility of pursuing an MBA online as well as on-campus to candidates worldwide. The MBA programme here is designed for 23 months, which is further broken down into two modules. Candidates will be studying for the first 11 months with the IMT Ghaziabad via online mode, and for the next 12 months, they need to attend the classes physically at the university’s campus in the US. The fee structure is different for both modules. Furthermore, it’s an excellent programme approach that allows candidates to join the on-campus classes for merely a year instead of pursuing a full-time MBA in a foreign country for a longer duration.

Flexible Learning:

The online MBA programme allows candidates flexibility in learning curriculum where students can learn at their own pace during the online mode. Also, flexibility in geographical locations is a major benefit for students who cannot shift to another location for higher studies.

Budget-Friendly:

Pursuing a full-time MBA from a foreign country requires not only a huge amount of fees but living expenses as well. Clarkson University allows participants to learn online for the first 11 months and demands to pursue a full-time MBA for just a year, which not only reduces the fee of eleven-month full-time courses abroad but also cuts down on the living expenses that a candidate has to pay when living outside.

Well Recognised Certifications:

Candidates will be rewarded an AGMP certificate from IMT Ghaziabad for accomplishing the learning programme of 11 months and get a well-recognised STEM MBA degree from Clarkson, US after completing the on-campus module.

More Job Opportunities:

Having an online MBA from a well-known institute along with a foreign degree on your CV not only opens the door to working in MNCs in your home country but also allows you to work abroad on higher packages in numerous job roles.

The educational and exam criteria differ depending on the 11-month and 12-month modules. Below are the essential requirements to be eligible to enrol in this course.

Educational Criteria: Candidates having a bachelor’s degree in any discipline with a minimum of 60% marks are eligible to enrol in the 11-month programme (online module from IMT Ghaziabad). However, to complete the second or 12-month module (on-campus at Clarkson University), the participants must qualify for the Advanced General Management Programme of the IMT Ghaziabad with an aggregate of 60% and above. Additionally, students are required to have 60% marks in each course of AGMP.

Entrance Exams: Enrolling in the online MBA programme does not require any test or exam, although, submitting the scores of any one of the English Proficiency Tests is mandatory to be eligible for studying in the US. The minimum scores based on different exams are as follows- TOEFL (iBT) >= 80, PTE >= 56,IELTS >= 6.5 Duolingo >= 115

Choosing an appropriate specialisation in an MBA course is critical since it determines the advancements in the career path. Here are some of the main reasons that can make you understand how selecting the right specialisation impacts your life as well as your future:

Practical Skills:

Every employer looks for an employee capable of performing specific goals required within their organisation. Practical experience in an area familiarises you with all positions and enables you to produce quality work to meet the set goals of the firm.

Higher Payscale:

Those who have an MBA tend to earn better salaries than those with only a bachelor's degree. The MBA graduates therefore are expected to earn much higher salaries throughout their employment, however, the pay scales vary depending on the specialties.

Career Growth:

Choosing the right specialisation to study in the MBA programme is crucial to your advancement in the job market. Specific specialisation is seen to be one’s territory and the majority of people get their preferred jobs in their chosen specialisations, which serves as the upward mobility chain of their careers.

Long-Term Success:

Each of the participants has generic knowledge, however, each candidate has their own area of interest in a particular field and, therefore, can be considered an expert concerning the chosen subject. This can be particularly handy in the long run.
