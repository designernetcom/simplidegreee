Executive Programme in Project Management

The Executive Programme in Project Management is a 6 to 12 month tailored course for mid and senior-level professionals. Focused on modern project management perspectives, it covers planning, financial control, risk mitigation, and advanced skills. The programme aligns with latest industry standards and aims to enhance employability of the candidate at a higher rank than before. This course integrates a combination of on-campus learning, live lectures, workshops, real-world cases, quizzes, and capstone projects.

Job Roles Job Description
Project Manager Leads and oversees all phases of projects, ensuring successful planning, execution, and completion within defined constraints of time and budget. Collaborates with the team and stakeholders, managing risks and issues.
Scrum Manager Facilitates the agile development team, sharing project progress, and planning for the next phase.
Project Management Consultant Administers all aspects of the project lifecycle from planning, development, implementation to assessment. Ensures timely completion of project deliverables.
Agile Project Manager Implements agile methodologies to enhance project flexibility, collaboration, and adaptability to changing requirements. Can assume roles as a consultant and coach.
Project Director A high-ranking position in the project management sector. Manages big-picture planning and oversees all running projects of the company.
