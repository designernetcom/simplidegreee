Madurai Kamaraj University Directorate of Distance Education

Madurai Kamaraj University is a Statutory University, established in 1965 by Govt. of Tamilnadu. It is recognised and funded by UGC and is a member of the Association of Indian Universities. The University is accorded with the status of University with Potential for Excellence - A status conferred by the University Grants Commission. The university is committed to the vision of providing higher education by disseminating and advancing knowledge through teaching, research and extension. The university stands true to its motto of “To seek Truth is Knowledge.” It provides distance learning under its DDE wing which is a flexible learning opportunity for students. All the programmes at DDE are crafted as per the current industry demands in the country which aims to provide high-class educational facilities to realise the potentials of learners and to strengthen the Nation's human resources.
