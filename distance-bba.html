About Distance BBA Course

Distance BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) is a three-year undergraduate programme popularly chosen by a huge pool of students. In this mode of learning, the college/university provides self-study material and recorded videos to students who can learn at their own pace. Some colleges often conduct in-person doubt clearing sessions for the distance learning students. This course covers the core business areas such as Marketing, Finance, Human Resource, and Operations among others. If you an interest in any particular discipline of the business field, you can add a specialisation to your degree. The focus of this degree is to train students for varioius business management roles and understand the nitty-gritty of business.

Versatile Degree

BBA degree is a mix of theory and practical application of the concepts of all the core areas in this field. You can learn multiple skills in one degree, therefore, preparing you for diverse job roles as per your choice.

Affordable Course

Among all the study modes of BBA, distance learning is the most affordable. Since it is based on a self-paced learning model, there are no hidden expenses when you pursue this degree.

Higher Salary Potential

Pursuing BBA prepares you for management and administrative roles where the salary range is high compared to other positions. Moreover, the chance to get promoted is also favorable in this industry.

Ease of Learning

As there are no physical classes and tight lecture schedules, you have the flexibility to learn as per your personal choice. It is especially beneficial for students from remote areas where internet connectivity is not good.

Choosing a specialization is like giving direction to your career. Here we have explained the importance of the right specialization for your career:

Interest and Passion

A specialization reflects your interests, and when we work on something we love, we stay motivated to give our 100%. Choosing to specialize in your area of interest will keep you motivated and will show you the path of improvement.

Market Demand

You should specialize in the domain which has high market demand. This way you can look for job opportunities in a growing sector. This brings job security and assured growth in the corporate sector.

Better Career Opportunities

While looking for skilled professionals, employers prefer candidates with niche skills and a strong understanding of the key concepts. This is where adding specialization to your BBA degree pays off.

Business is a vast field with multiple avenues. Adding this degree to your qualification will make you eligibile for various career opportunities. Though the job role and position depends on the specialisation you choose and your skills, here are few popularly chosen career paths:

Job Roles Job Description
Sales Executive An individual who is responsible for boosting the company's sales while maintaining a client base. They are a key point of contact for their customers.
Business Development Executive A professional whose role is to help the company identify and develop new business opportunities. The duties revolve around searching potential clients and helping the company expand into new markets.
Business Consultant Their role and responsibilities include an array of services and vary upon the type of industry. From the legal and finance department to the marketing domain, business consultants provide professional advice in various sectors depending upon their niche.
Human Resource Executive In this position, you will have to perform regular tasks such as recruiting new talent, conducting interviews, and overseeing employee salaries while keeping in check the company policies.
Business Analyst Their job is to carry out research and analyze raw data to enhance the efficiency of business functions. Most of their work is done in collaboration with IT professionals.
Marketing Executive This job role involves planning, developing, and overseeing the marketing strategies of the company. Once the marketing campaign is executed, he/she has to monitor its performance and record the results for future reference.
