Centre for Distance Education, Anna University

Anna University is a unitary type of institution established in 1978 offering higher education in Engineering, Technology, Architecture and Applied Sciences relevant to the current and projected needs of the society. In line with the growing global trend of extending conventional university instructional mode to online, self learning and distance education methodology, Anna University had created the Centre for Distance Education. The curriculum involves E-learning teaching aids and comprehensive coursework. The university has a various study centres in and around Chennai to provide administrative services.

Courses Eligibility
Distance MBA Candidates must have qualified the three year or four year Bachelor’s degree examination after passing 10+2 or equivalent under Indian board or foreign board or 8+4 or 9+3 under foreign board. Additionally, in some specializations, candidates seeking admission to MBA program are required to qualify in the Online Education Entrance Test (OEET) conducted by Anna University.
Distance MCA Must have completed BCA/bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering or equivalent degree with atleast 50% marks (45% incase of reserved category candidates) Must have passed Distance Education Entrance Test (DEET) conducted by CDE, Anna University, Chennai
Distance MSc Must have completed bachelors degree after 10+2 or equivalent. The candidate should have studied Mathematics/Statistics as one of the subjects at the degree level or Mathematics at 10+2 level.
