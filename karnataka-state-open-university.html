Karnataka State Open University

Karnataka State Open University (KSOU) stands as a pioneering institution in higher education, committed to deliver quality learning experiences through its innovative online and distance programmes. With its legacy dating back to its establishment in 1996, KSOU has upscaled the values of equity, quality, affordability, and accountability in higher education. As a center of Open and Distance Learning (ODL), KSOU satisfies the diverse needs of learners, providing them with the flexibility to pursue education from anywhere in the world. The university's recent achievement of an A+ rating in the NAAC accreditation reflects its commitment to academic excellence. The university's extensive infrastructure, comprising 21 regional centers and numerous affiliated colleges, ensures widespread access to quality education.

Fill the online application and fee payment personal and programme details after registration
STEP 3

STEP 4
After successful admission, log in to the LMS Application and start learning
