It is mandatory to meet the eligibility criteria for candidates looking forward to enrolling in the programme:

For Online Program: Educational Background: Students with a 3 or 4 year bachelor’s degree having a minimum of 55% marks are eligible to apply. Applications of students with a lower percentage in Bachelor’s degree will be evaluated based on their SOP and work experience.

For Transfer to On-Campus: Educational Background: The Student must successfully complete specific six modules/courses (18 Credits) of the online program with an aggregate of minimum 3.0 CGPA with a minimum passing grade of C- in each course English Proficiency Requirement : To be eligible for this course, an applicant must have appeared for one of the examinations and have scored as mentioned below: If appearing for TOEFL, then the score must be equal to or more than 79. If appearing for IELTS, then the score must be either equal to or more than 6.5. If appearing for Duolingo, then the score must be either equal to or more than 100. And if the learner will appear for the PTE English Language Test, then the score must be either equal to or more than 57.
