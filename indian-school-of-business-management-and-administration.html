Indian School of Business Management and Administration

ISBM was established in 1992 by a well-known NGOAeren Foundation. It is also ranked among the top ten ranked International B-Schools that provide management courses through correspondence. ISBM has over 100 specialisations and management courses to choose from ISBM is an NGO working its way to reaching towards people in the most distant corners of not only India but the world by providing correspondence courses in the management field, with specially designed courses for working professionals; from Diploma in management courses to the Doctorate and Laureate level. At ISBM, the courses are designed for those who have a burning desire to pursue a career in business and management. The students will get qualified without disrupting their current career progression and earnings.

Admission Process

The admissions process takes place in online mode. Fresh admission starts from the month of January of every year. There are direct admissions, no entrance exam is conducted for the admission process.The addmission procedure 2025 for the online course at Indian School of Business Management and Administration is as follow:

STEP 1
Download the application form from the website.
STEP 2
Choose and fill in the course name and electives from the list of available courses and several electives very carefully.
STEP 3
Send the scanned copies of your application form and complete the payment process.
STEP 4
Wait for admission confirmation.
