About the Course

Data analytics is a rapidaly growing field of the current time and someone is required in almost every industry. Owing to the high demand of the programme enrolling in online certifications has become very popular among aspiring candidates. The free certifications provided by various online platforms gives you a chance to develop a new skills or enhance you existing skills. These courses cover the foundational areas of data analytics which adds value to your resume and reflects credibiloity of your profile in the job market.

Students who complete this programme will have the skills necessary to analyse surplous data critically, understand it, visualise it, and transform it into a useful insights. Through this programme you get an overview of data science methodologies and can move forward to become data analyst in future. Here's a quick review of things you will learn in this certification:

Understanding of processes used by associate data analysts
How to clean and organize data
Get hands-on with spreadsheets, SQL, R Programming, Tableau
Fundamentals of data analytics
Types of data analytics methods
Frequency distribution plots
Swarm plots
Data visualization concepts
Emerging trends in data analytics
Analytics framework

The data analytics certifications does not require prior knowledge however, some portals do prefer candidates with basic knowledge of mathematics, programming or statistics. The certification is ideal for the following candidates:

Beginners who wish to enter this field
Graduation or post-graduation students
Tech professionals
Finance professionals
Sales professionals
On some platforms learning is free, however, to get the certificate, candidates have to appear for the exam.
There are portals which charge a minimal fee to receive the certification while the learning is free.
These certifications have self-paced videos which can be covered in 3hrs or more depending upon the course plan of the provider.
Few platforms give free access to the free course for a certain period of time.
