Job Roles Job Description
Investment Banker Advises clients on matters concerning investment, playing a crucial role in the financial growth of clients and their organization.
Credit Manager Administers the credit granting process by analyzing current and potential customers. Job titles or roles may differ at various organizations.
Financial Accountant In charge of keeping financial records, preparing statements, and analyzing financial data for the company. Reports information in balance sheets, cash flow statements, and other documents.
Financial Risk Manager Identifies financial threats, analyzes earning capacity, and works on other financial services tasks for the company.
Asset Manager Primarily responsible for managing clients' assets, studying market changes regularly, and suggesting potential changes to clients.
Financial Advisor Has multiple roles varying by industry and company. Uses expertise and knowledge to develop financial plans for clients.
