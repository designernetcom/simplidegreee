IGNOU

Founded in 1985, Indira Gandhi National Open University has risen to the mark of the largest university in the world in terms of enrollment. The university is regarded as the leader in distance education and was conferred with excellence awards by the Commonwealth of Learning (COL), Canada. It has a broad network of 69 regional centres and a strong international presence with 25 overseas study centres. IGNOU is the widely chosen government university for its distance and online learning programmes. Pursuing degrees from a central government institution adds higher credibility to a student’s profile. Under the online and distance learning centres, the university offers courses from diploma to PhD level.

Admission Process

The admissions process takes place in online mode. Fresh admission starts from the month of January of every year. There are direct admissions, no entrance exam is conducted for the admission process.The addmission procedure 2025 for the online course at IGNOU is as follow:

STEP 1
Register on the IGNOU Admission Portal and receive login credentials
STEP 2
Login using the ID/password to proceed with the application form
STEP 3
Once the application form is filled, pay the registration fee via credit card, net banking, and ATM cards
STEP 4
After checking all your information, click the submit button and complete the application procedure
