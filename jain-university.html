Jain University Online

Established in 1990, Jain University achieved the status of a "Deemed-to-be-University" from the UGC in 2009. Over the years, it has expanded to include 7 thriving campuses, with over 18,310 students enrolled across various programmes.

To cater to the evolving educational needs of students, the university launched its dedicated e-learning platform, Jain Online, which serves as a comprehensive solution for online education.
